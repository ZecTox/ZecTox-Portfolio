Frequently Asked Questions

The questions merchants usually ask before bringing in a Shopify developer.

Why hire a specialized Shopify Developer like Tejas Kedare?

Specialized developers ensure your store is optimized for speed, conversions, and scalability. Unlike generalists, I bring deep expertise in custom themes, Liquid programming, and e-commerce strategy to deliver enterprise-grade results.

Do you offer custom Shopify theme development?

Yes, I specialize in building pixel-perfect, high-converting custom themes tailored to your brand's unique identity. Whether you need a fresh design or a complete overhaul, I create stores that stand out and sell more.

Can you help with Shopify speed optimization?

Absolutely. I optimize code, images, and app integrations to ensure lightning-fast load times. A faster store improves SEO rankings, reduces bounce rates, and significantly boosts conversion rates.

What is your experience with E-commerce migration?

I have extensive experience migrating stores from platforms like Wix, WordPress, and WooCommerce to Shopify. I ensure a seamless transition with zero data loss, preserving your SEO rankings and customer history.

Do you provide ongoing support after the store launch?

Yes, I offer post-launch support and maintenance packages. From regular updates and bug fixes to adding new features, I ensure your store continues to run smoothly and grows with your business.
